# GraFx Genie Product Image Composer

![rn_icon](/assets/icon-Grafx-Genie.svg){.rn_icon}

## What it is

**GraFx Genie Product Image Composer** builds product-shot compositions by combining products, backgrounds, and effects into a single scene — a guided, step-based way to create professional product imagery without external tools.

## How GraFx Genie helps

Genie assembles the scene for you across a few steps: choose an aspect ratio, set a background (upload, browse GraFx Media, or generate with AI), add product shots (upload, GraFx Media, or AI), and apply special effects such as bokeh, lens flare, caustics, or volumetric lighting. You stay on-brand by pulling approved assets from GraFx Media, while Genie handles the composition and rendering.
