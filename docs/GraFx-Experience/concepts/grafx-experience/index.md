# GraFx Experience

GraFx Experience is a branded portal platform that gives end users access to on-brand content. Through the portal, they can browse available Smart Templates, personalize them within defined brand rules, and produce output — without needing design skills or access to GraFx Studio.

## Who sets it up

The portal is set up and configured by CHILI publish. Customers receive access to a ready-to-use environment. The portal structure, branding, and available content are all managed on their behalf.

## What end users do in the portal

- Browse available Smart Templates
- Open a template and personalize allowed content (text, images, colors)
- Produce output (download, export, or send to a production channel)

## How Smart Templates get into the portal

Smart Templates from GraFx Studio are made available through the **CHILI GraFx extension**. The extension is the bridge between GraFx Experience and CHILI GraFx.

See [CHILI GraFx extension](../chili-grafx-extension/) for details.
